## About the book

*Beneath the Shell* is a published work by Kalyan Gopalam, written alongside his
academic and security-research work. It reflects the same hands-on, systems-level
approach that runs through the rest of this site — looking past the surface of a
tool or a system to understand how it really works, and where it breaks.
